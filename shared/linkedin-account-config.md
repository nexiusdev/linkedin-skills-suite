## Detection Instructions

**To detect your account type:**

1. Go to linkedin.com/premium
2. Check what's shown:
   - "Get Premium" button = FREE
   - "Manage Premium" = PREMIUM (check which tier)
   - Sales Navigator icon in nav = SALES_NAVIGATOR

3. Update the Account Status table above
